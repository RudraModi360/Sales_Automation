# Tecblic Case Studies: Sector-Wise Project Summary

our few of recent projects are as mentioend as below :

## 1. Agriculture (Agri-Commodity & Finance)
### Project: Crop2Cash
- **Problem:** Manual inventory discrepancies, lack of real-time tracking, and high operational costs in warehouse management.
- **Solution:** An AI-driven ERP system using **IoT** for real-time monitoring and **Machine Learning** for predictive inventory control.
- **Impact:** 30% increase in efficiency and 40% reduction in operational costs.

## 2. Manufacturing
### Project: AI-Inspect & Predictive Maintenance
- **Problem:** Human error in food safety inspections (airline catering) and costly downtime due to unexpected machine failures.
- **Solution:** - **AI-Inspect:** Dual-camera (Thermal + RGB) computer vision system for automated quality checks.
  - **Predictive Maintenance:** IoT sensor integration with **Azure Databricks** to forecast equipment failures.
- **Impact:** 84% reduction in maintenance costs and enhanced food safety compliance.

## 3. BFSI (Banking & Finance)
### Project: FinGuard AI & OCR Invoice Processing
- **Problem:** Sluggish risk assessment for loan defaults and manual, error-prone invoice data entry.
- **Solution:** - **FinGuard:** Automated data pipelines on **Snowflake/AWS** for real-time risk prediction.
  - **OCR:** Template-independent AI extraction to automate financial document processing.
- **Impact:** Real-time financial risk assessments and significant reduction in manual labor.

## 4. Healthcare
### Project: MediBot
- **Problem:** Lack of accessible, reliable preliminary medical guidance for users.
- **Solution:** A **Generative AI (GPT-3)** chatbot designed for symptom analysis and preliminary health assessments.
- **Impact:** Reduced unnecessary hospital visits and provided 24/7 reliable health insights.

## 5. Retail & E-Commerce
### Project: HoloBox (AI + AR)
- **Problem:** Low customer engagement with traditional static marketing and advertisements.
- **Solution:** An **Augmented Reality** platform (ARKit/ARCore) for 3D product visualization and interactive brand experiences.
- **Impact:** 30% increase in customer engagement and 25% boost in sales conversion.

## 6. Technology & Design
### Project: CANVA8
- **Problem:** Time-consuming manual conversion of design mockups into HTML/CSS code.
- **Solution:** AI-powered **Computer Vision and LLMs** to automatically detect UI elements and generate clean, responsive code.
- **Impact:** 100% reduction in repetitive manual coding tasks.
